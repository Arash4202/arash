Satoru Gojo

五ご条じょう悟さとる

At his high school in Sendai, Yuji Itadori avoids the athletics club and prefers to go to the occult club. There the actually sporty student can relax with the other two members Sasaki and Iguchi and leave school early to visit his sick grandfather. When he dies soon afterwards, he tells his grandson to help everyone and not die alone. Yuji is then visited by Megumi Fushiguro, a student at the Municipal Technical High School for Magic. He is looking for an amulet that has a curse on it. But Sasaki and Iguchi took the finger out of the amulet and broke the seal of the curse. Inside the amulet lies a withered finger
